I started Weekly OverVue, because I'd been looking for active blogs covering standalone, community Vue/Nuxt 3 components, plugins, themes, etc. in action. Or in other words - their potential use-cases in actual applications. But as you've probably guessed by now - I'd failed to find any blogs that would match the criteria.

Weekly OverVue aims to do so by regularly providing articles guiding you through the development process of projects which are built around a certain third-party component. That way, not only are you given a demonstration of its capabilities in a real-life scenario, but also a potential baseline for your future Vue or Nuxt project.

Lastly - each post is available on Hashnode too, which means you won't have to worry about losing access to any Weekly OverVue article should this page become unavailable for whatever reason. So what are you waiting for? Check out the latest articles down below, and make sure to sign up for the newsletter so as not to miss out on any new articles!
