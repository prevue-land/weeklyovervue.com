About

Hey! My name is Maciej Pędzich, but you can call me Mac. I started Weekly OverVue, because I'd been looking for active blogs covering standalone, community Vue/Nuxt components, plugins, themes, etc. in action. Or in other words - their potential use-cases in actual applications. But as you've probably guessed by now - I'd failed to find any blogs that would match the criteria.

Weekly OverVue aims to do so by regularly providing articles guiding you through the development process of projects which are built around a certain third-party component. That way, not only are you given a demonstration of its capabilities in a real-life scenario, but also a potential baseline for your future Vue or Nuxt project. This includes suggestions regarding its tech stack, as well as a list of features required for an MVP.

Lastly - each post is available on Hashnode too, which means you won't have to worry about losing access to any Weekly OverVue article should this page become unavailable for whatever reason.
